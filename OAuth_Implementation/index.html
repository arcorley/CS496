Project Description:

This project seeks to demonstrate the process of authorizing a user via Google's OAuth 2.0 endpoint.
On this page, you will need to click the button below to begin the process of authorizing with Google.
After this is completed, you will be taken to Google's endpoint, where you will be asked to sign in with your Google credentials.
Once this is complete, you will be redirected back to this site, where your name will be displayed on the screen.
